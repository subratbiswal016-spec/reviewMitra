✨ ReviewMitra AI

The ultimate guide to setting up your AI-Powered Google Reviews Assistant

📦 Part 1: How to Install the Extension

1

Download & Unzip

Locate the ReviewMitra.zip file you received. Right-click the file and select Extract All (on Windows) or double-click it (on Mac) to unzip it. Remember where you saved this new unzipped folder (like your Desktop).

2

Open Chrome Extensions

Open Google Chrome. Click the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner of your browser. Go to Extensions > Manage Extensions.

Alternatively, you can just copy and paste this link into your address bar:

chrome://extensions/
3

Turn on Developer Mode

Look at the very top right corner of the Extensions page. You will see a toggle switch labeled Developer mode. Click the switch to turn it ON.

4

Load the Extension

Once Developer mode is ON, a new menu bar will appear at the top left. Click the button that says Load unpacked. A file window will open. Find the folder you unzipped in Step 1, select the dist folder inside it, and click Select Folder. You're done!

🚀 Part 2: How to Use ReviewMitra

1

Go to Google Maps

Open Google Maps and search for your business. Click on the Reviews tab to see what your customers have written about you.

2

Click the Magic Button

Under every single customer review, you will now magically see a new button that says ✨ ReviewMitra AI. Click it!

3

Choose the Best Draft

The AI will instantly read the customer's review and generate 3 highly professional, personalized responses for you. Choose the draft you like best.

4

Copy & Paste

Click the 📋 Clipboard Icon in the corner of your favorite draft to instantly copy it. Finally, click Google's standard "Reply" button and paste it in! You just saved 10 minutes of typing!

💡 Secret Pro Tip: Custom Instructions

Do you want the AI to mention something very specific? Type instructions in the small text box (for example, "Mention that we fixed the broken AC" or "Sign off as Mandeep, the General Manager") and click Regenerate. The AI will instantly rewrite all the drafts to follow your exact instructions!
